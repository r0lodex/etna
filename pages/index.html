What is ETNA?

E-Training Needs Assessment or ETNA is a system that provides comprehensive analysis of the skillsets and compentency level of employees in a company. The solution will ensure that the human resources and workforce within the company is on par with the current industry trends and market's needs.

Who are the users of ETNA?

If you are looking forward to improve your skill to take a step further in your career then ETNA is the solution! But we suggest all companies in Malaysia will register their company with our system to helps us provide much more details report for our current human resources competency and skillsets levels for each industry. And no worries as this system is approved by the Human Resources Development Fund (HRDF) to provide much needed training courses as the industry needs.
